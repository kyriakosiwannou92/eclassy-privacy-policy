eClassy Teacher – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how eClassy Teacher (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application for teachers and institute administrators.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: name, phone number, and email address
  • Professional data: classes, students, attendance, homework, announcements, payment reminders, and institute membership
  • Parent contact data: parent phone numbers and link status when you invite or manage parent access (used to notify parents via the eClassy Class app)
  • Device information: FCM token, platform (iOS/Android), and app version
  • Usage data: sign-in activity and in-app notification history

3. How we use your information

  • To manage classes, students, homework, announcements, and attendance
  • To send notifications to you and, where applicable, to linked parents through eClassy Class
  • To operate institute features (registration IDs, teacher approvals, subscriptions)
  • To keep the service secure and reliable

4. Push notifications

  • We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for teacher alerts
  • Parents receive separate notifications on the eClassy Class app
  • You can disable notifications in the app (Settings) or in device system settings

5. Data sharing

  • Student and parent contact information is shared only as needed for school communication between teachers, institutes, and linked parents
  • We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and messaging
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties

6. Data retention and deletion

  • You can delete your account from Settings → Delete account, which anonymizes your personal account data on our servers
  • Class and student records may be retained for legitimate school administration or legal requirements

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us using the details below.

8. Your responsibilities

As a teacher or institute user, you should only enter parent/student information you are authorised to use and in line with your school’s policies and applicable law.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit and on our servers. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.
